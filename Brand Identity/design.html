Design

Our design principles are the guiding values that shape every aspect of our design system, from the smallest button to the overall user experience. They serve as a compass for our design decisions, ensuring that every element we create is not only visually appealing but also functional, inclusive, and aligned with our brand's mission.

Brand Overview

Lunch Money’s brand is built around clarity, friendliness, and calm financial confidence. The product helps people manage their money without stress, so the visual identity reflects simplicity, warmth, and trust. Every element from color to typography to spacing supports a user experience that feels approachable, modern, and reassuring.

Color System

Lunch Money’s color system uses a balanced palette designed for clarity and emotional comfort. The primary colors establish the core brand identity, combining a bright, optimistic yellow with a grounded dark neutral for text and structure. Secondary colors provide flexibility for charts, categories, and UI accents, adding personality without overwhelming the interface. Semantic colors (success, warning, error, info) communicate meaning quickly and consistently, ensuring users always understand the state of their finances at a glance. All colors are chosen for accessibility, contrast, and readability across light and dark surfaces.

Typography

Lunch Money uses the Inter typeface for its clean, modern, and highly readable character. The typography system is built on a clear hierarchy: from bold, confident headings to simple, legible body text. Consistent line‑height, spacing, and weight rules ensure that text feels open and approachable, never crowded or intimidating. This type system reinforces the brand’s personality: friendly, trustworthy, and easy to understand, even when dealing with complex financial information.
